Feline Funkalicious

Born during the Big Jam, a mythical moment when the universe was still young and every star was in sync with the funky beat of creation, Feline Funkalicious came into existence as a living manifestation of music. Legend has it that they were born in the heart of a supernova, the result of cosmic forces colliding in perfect harmony—a stellar event so explosive and groovy that it birthed the essence of funk itself. From the moment they took their first cosmic step, Feline Funkalicious has been on an eternal tour, spreading rhythm and soul across dimensions, galaxies, and worlds in need of a little groove.

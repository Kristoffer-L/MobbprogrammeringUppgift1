The Rolling Yarns

Led by a charismatic and fearless frontcat, Mick Jagpaw, The Rolling Yarns formed with one simple goal: to live life to the fullest and leave behind a legacy of stories so wild that they’d be remembered for generations. The Rolling Yarns aren’t just musicians—they are chroniclers of adventure. Every song they play, every riff they strum, and every beat they drum is inspired by the adventures they’ve lived.

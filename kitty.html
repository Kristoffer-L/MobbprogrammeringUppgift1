Kitty Van Halen

Kitty Van Halen wasn't just any ordinary cat. Legend has it that he was born during a legendary rock festival that took place on the edge of the Thundermountain, where the skies split open, and rain fell in the form of pure molten sound. His very first breath was filled with the thunderous vibrations of guitars, drums, and screaming vocals, and from that moment, it was clear that Kitty was destined for greatness. His earliest days were spent playing around amps and speaker stacks, chasing down the echoes of legendary guitar riffs that reverberated across the lands. Even as a kitten, he had an innate gift for rhythm and a burning desire to harness the raw power of sound.
